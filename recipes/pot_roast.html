Slow Cooker Pot Roast

Description

This slow cooker pot roast always turns out moist and juicy. It's cooked with carrots, onion, celery, and potatoes. This slow cooker pot roast is tender, juicy, and flavorful. Plus, it's the perfect low-maintenance meal for cozy nights at home!

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds chuck roast: A four-pound chuck roast should make about eight servings.
  • Salt and pepper to taste: This slow cooker pot roast recipe is simply seasoned with salt and pepper.
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil: Use olive oil to sear the chuck roast to brown on each side.
  • 1 packet dry onion soup mix: A package of dry onion soup mix adds tons of flavor.
  • 1 cup water: A cup of water keeps the slow cooker pot roast from drying out.
  • Vegetables: 3 carrots (chopped), 3 potatoes (peeled and cubed), 1 onion (chopped), and 1 stalk celery (chopped) add substance and make it a meal.

Steps

  1. Season and sear the chuck roast.
  2. Place the ingredients in the slow cooker.
  3. Cook on Low for 8 to 10 hours.

Serve with your favorite sides for any additional flare you'd like to add to this fantastic meal.
